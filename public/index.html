Hello, I’m a computer science researcher interested in Equitable AI, Human-Computer Interaction, and Accessibility. Currently, I’m in my final year of a PhD in Computer Science at Computational Foundry, Swansea University. I work with my fantastic advisors: Dr. Matt Jones, Dr. Simon Robinson, Dr. Jennifer Pearson, and Dr. Cecily Morrison (Microsoft Research, Cambridge). My PhD thesis explores “disability-first approaches to AI dataset collection in low-resource communities,” and is funded by EPSRC and Microsoft Research, Cambridge. I’m always excited to discuss new ideas (and currently exploring full-time roles!), so feel free to reach out if you’d like to chat about my work.
